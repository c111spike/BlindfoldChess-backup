To make this bot truly dangerous, it needs to be obsessed with Clearance.

The Tweak: If the bot has two Bishops and the opponent has one or zero, add a Global Evaluation Bonus (+40).

The Why: The bot will refuse to trade one of its Bishops for a Knight, even if the material is technically equal. It will fight to keep "The Pair" into the endgame.

The Why: This forces the bot to sacrifice a pawn or trade it off just to "unleash" the Bishop.

## 🐎 The "Knight Lover" (The Octopus)
A Knight Specialist needs to play for Suffocation.

1. The "Octopus" Knight (7th/6th Rank)
A Knight on the 6th rank is often worth more than a Rook.

The Tweak: Double the Outpost Bonus (+100) if the Knight reaches the 6th rank (c6,d6,e6,f6).

The Why: Once the Knight lands here, it paralyzes the opponent. The bot should prioritize getting a Knight to d6 over almost anything else.

2. The "Anchor" Pawn Logic
An outpost is only good if it's permanent.

The Tweak: Bonus (+30) for a Pawn supporting a Knight on a central square.

The Why: This prevents the "Knight Lover" from jumping into an outpost where it can just be kicked away by a pawn. It will only jump when the "Anchor" is ready.

3. The "Fork" Vision
Knights are the kings of tactical geometry.

The Tweak: Increase the weight of Discovery/Fork tactics by 1.5x for this bot.

The Why: The bot will actively seek positions where the Queen and King are on the same color squares, even if a fork isn't immediately visible, just to increase the possibility of a Knight jump.
