Why your "Genius" detector is misfiring:
The "Ghost" Sacrifice: If a piece is attacked twice and defended once, and you move something else, your code might flag that as a "sacrifice" because the piece is now "hanging." In reality, you just blundered or made a standard trade.

Trade-Downs: If you trade a Queen for a Queen, the material count drops by 9. If your code just looks for "Material Change < 0," it will flag every single trade in the game as a sacrifice.

The "Already Hanging" Loophole: If your piece was already being attacked before the move, and you didn't move it, your logic might be saying: "Look! He's still sacrificing it! Genius!"
