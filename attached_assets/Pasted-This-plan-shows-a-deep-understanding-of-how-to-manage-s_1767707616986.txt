🕵️ Why "The" is the most important fixIn Android's speech-to-text engine, "D" is often treated as a "stop word" or an article. If a user says "D" in isolation, the engine's language model assumes they meant "The" because it's a more statistically probable sentence starter. Mapping the $\rightarrow$ d is the #1 way to make a chess app feel "native" on Android.🚀 Pro-Tip for "No Match" RecoveryYou mentioned applying the fix to the "no match found" path. This is vital.
