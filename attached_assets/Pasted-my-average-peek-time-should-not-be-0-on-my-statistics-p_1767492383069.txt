Based on the code analysis, there are two reasons why your statistics page shows "0" for the average peek time, while your game review shows the correct average.

The Problem: Unit Mismatch
The Game Review page (client-side) correctly treats the peek time as seconds. However, the Statistics page (server-side) assumes the database stores it in milliseconds and divides it by 1000.

Example: If your average peek time is 5 seconds.

Game Review: Reads 5. Displays 5s. (Correct)

Statistics Page: Reads 5. Divides by 1000 → 0.005. Rounds to 0. (Incorrect)

The Fix
You need to modify server/storage.ts to stop dividing by 1000. I also recommend applying the query fix we discussed earlier to ensure "Perfect Games" (0 peeks) are counted in the average.
