Those numbers tell a very specific story: you’ve cleared the "JavaScript Traffic Jam," but now you’ve hit a "Main Thread Bottleneck."

On mobile (Slow 4G), your TBT is a perfect 10ms, which is incredible. However, your FCP (7.9s) and LCP (9.1s) are high because the browser is waiting for the CSS and Fonts to "unlock" the screen so it can paint.

The "Smoking Gun" in your Report
Lighthouse is showing a massive 1.2-second "Element Render Delay." This means the browser has already downloaded your hero image, but it's sitting there with the image in memory, refusing to show it because it's still busy parsing your CSS.

The Final "Green Score" Hit List
1. The "Logo" Resize (The "Easy" 5 Points)
Your SimulChess Logo is currently 96 KB but only displayed at 32x32.

The Problem: You're forcing a mobile phone to download a giant image just to shrink it to the size of a thumbnail.

The Fix: Convert it to an SVG or a 32x32 WebP. It should be < 2 KB.

2. Critical CSS Inlining (The "FCP" Winner)
Your index.css is 117 KB, and it's blocking the entire page.

The Problem: The browser won't show any text or images until that 117 KB file is fully processed.

The Fix: If you are using Tailwind, ensure you are using the minify flag in your build.

Pro Move: Take the CSS for just the "Hero" section (the top of your landing page) and put it inside a <style> tag in your index.html. Load the rest of the CSS with rel="preload" and an onload handler.

3. Hero Image: JPG vs. WebP
Lighthouse is still flagging your hero image (chess_tournament_pro...jpg) for "Modern Image Formats."

The Fix: Convert that 142 KB JPG to a WebP at 70% quality. It will likely drop to ~40 KB, which will shave a full second off your LCP on 4G.

4. The Font "Preload"
Your report shows a 750ms delay just waiting for the Google Font stylesheet.

The Fix: Since you only have 2 fonts now, don't just preconnect. Preload the actual .woff2 file for your "Inter" font in your <head>:

HTML
<link rel="preload" href="/fonts/inter-v20-latin-regular.woff2" as="font" type="font/woff2" crossorigin>
