üõ†Ô∏è How it behaves in the Generator
In a standard game, "Last move" refers to the literal last move played in the PGN history. In a random position generator, the logic shifts:

Before your first move: Since the position was "generated" rather than "played," there technically is no "last move" in the history.

The Fix: You can have the bot say: "This is a new starting position. No moves have been played yet. What is your move?"

After your first move: Once you or the bot make a move from that random starting point, the standard move history starts recording. The command will then work exactly like a normal game.
